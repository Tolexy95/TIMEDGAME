* Welcome to the Word Guessing Game!

*You will have 5 chances per round to guess a word within 30 minutes.

*If either the chances or timer expires, the game will stop

* The 30-minute timer will start immediately when you click the play button.

* Enter a letter or guess the entire word when prompted with the hint.

* If you enter a letter,if the letter is in the secret word, the positions of the letter will be revealed to you and the displayed word will be updated accordingly.

* If your guess matches the secret word, you win the round and the game ends.

* If your guess does not match the secret word, you lose the round and the game ends.

* Your final score will be displayed, indicating the number of rounds you won.

*Click on instruction link to navigate back to start button
